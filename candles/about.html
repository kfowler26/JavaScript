About

Kendall’s Candles was started in 2015 by Kendall Fowler in Regina, Saskatchewan. A few years prior Kendall was becoming sick and tired of only being able to buy overly perfumed, mass-produced candles at big box stores so she decided to try and make her own.

After ordering a soy candle making kit online she found herself a new hobby, once she got the knack of it she began handing her homemade candles out as gifts for holidays, birthdays, and housewarming parties. This is where the real trouble began. Her candles were a big hit and people began requesting them to be custom made. Once her hobby began to overtake her life, she decided to give up her full-time day job and open the Kendall’s Candles online store.

When Kendall isn’t overseeing the daily operations of Kendall’s Candles you can find her travelling with her husband or walking her two dogs Belle and Poco.
