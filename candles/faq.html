Frequently Asked Questions

What are these candles made of?

Kendall’s Candles are handcrafted from 100% soy, cotton wicks, natural dyes and chemical-free natural essential oils.

Are Kendall’s Candles vegan?

Kendall does not test the candles on animals, not sure how she could do that anyway. They also contain no animal products in the wax, essential oils, or wick.

How do I burn Kendall’s Candles ?

Start by lighting them on fire, then burn 3-4 hours each time for maximum scent release and even wax levels. Please remember to cut the candle wick to 1/4 inch each time before lighting.

If I don't like the candles can I return them?

If you are at all unsatisfied with your order please do not hesitate to contact us at info@kendallscandles.com.

Can Kendall’s Candles be shipped anywhere?

We offer free shipping within Canada and offer competitive prices to ship to the USA. We are able tp provide a quote for international shipping if required.
